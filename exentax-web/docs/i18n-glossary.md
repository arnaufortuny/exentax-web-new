## 5. Common false friends to AVOID

- **EN "actually"** ≠ ES "actualmente" → use "currently" / "now".
- **FR "éventuellement"** = "possibly", not "eventually".
- **DE "aktuell"** = "current(ly)", not "actually".
- **PT "atualmente"** = "currently", not "actually".
- **EN "comprehensive"** ≠ "comprensivo" → use "exhaustivo / completo".
- **EN "advice"** is uncountable; ES "consejo / asesoramiento" → never "advices".

## 6. Formatting conventions

- LLC names always in upper case, no periods (`LLC`, not `L.L.C.`).
- Tax form numbers in upper case with a non-breaking space:
  `Form 5472`, `Form 1120`, `W-8BEN`.
- Currency: `$` before amount in EN; `€` after amount in ES/CA/FR/PT;
  `€` after amount in DE with thin space (`1.000 €`).
- Decimal: `,` in ES/CA/FR/DE/PT; `.` in EN.
- Thousands separator: `.` in ES/CA/DE/PT; non-breaking space in FR;
  `,` in EN.
